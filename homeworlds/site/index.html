Babamots' Homeworlds Site

This is a fan site for the board game Homeworlds by John Cooper and published by Looney Labs.

In particular, this site is about Binary Homeworlds, the two player version.

My site is intended for people with a basic understanding of Homeworlds. If that's not you, you may want to look at the rules or tutorial videos listed on Looney Labs' Homeworlds index page.

My strategy guide is the best feature of this site. Enjoy :-)

My implementation on Board Game Arena is probably the best place to play online these days.
  • Unfortunately, BGA doesn't let you rewind smoothly when reviewing games. The best place to review a game visually is the Homeworlds Live sandbox.
  • You can use this neat tool I made to convert BGA's game log to HWL format.

If you would like to support Homeworlds financially, the first thing you should do is buy a copy, either as part of Pyramid Arcade or the standalone Pyramid Quartet box .

I usually buy directly from Looney Labs, but they would encourage you to buy from a local retail store.

If you would like to support me, who am I to say no? I've put lots of work into organizing tournaments, building this site, and writing the BGA implementation.
