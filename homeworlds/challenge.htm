Variants:
Hard Time
 
This variant will auto-surrender players that time out. It is totally unforgiving, so be careful when playing such games. Zendo games are unaffected by this variant.
No Undo
 
This variant disables the Undo button (in games that have said feature of course).
Sinister
 
This variant is a 3-4 player alternative to the default "Last Man Standing" end-of-game condition. Here, the player that eliminates the player to his left is victorious.
Unrated
 
Selecting this variant will prevent the outcome from affecting your ELO rating.
